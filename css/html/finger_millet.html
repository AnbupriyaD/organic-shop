Description

Finger millet, or ragi, is a nutritious gluten-free grain rich in calcium, iron, protein, and fiber. It supports bone health, aids in digestion, regulates blood sugar levels, and promotes heart health. Additionally, its antioxidants improve skin health and boost cognitive function.
